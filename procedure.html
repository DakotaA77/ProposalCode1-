Questions I Would Ask

Do you identify with being a woman or falling outside of the gender binary?

This question allows me to make sure the participant falls within our participant guidelines.This question also allows space for the participant to self describe their relationship to gender

Do you know what gendered language is?

This question would open up the conversation and allow me to record the participants idea of what gendered language is or give a definition for gendered language if they are not familiar with the term. Simple Definition: Gendered language refers to any form of language which implies the gender identity of the person it is referring to

Have you experienced the use of gendered language at PSU? In classrooms or around campus? Spoken or written out?

This question allows participants to identify the use of gendered language in their academic space including possibly pinpointing specific times that gendered language stood out to them

Do you feel like this gendered language can be discouraging? Is it discouraging for you?

This question helps to answer our question of “Is gendered language discouraging in academic spaces for women and those outside of the gender binary?

Could you give a specific example of how gendered language impacts you?

This question helps to record the specific impacts of gendered language on these students

All of these questions are base-line questions which could segway into follow up questions to record more data if necessary or if the participant is wanting to share further.

A control is not necessary for this research because I am not comparing experiences, but instead collecting data on the experience of specific people.

Procedure

I would like to promote this study using on campus locations where women and nonbinary students may be more likely to hear about it; the women’s resource center and the queer resource center.

The participants would most likely be volunteer based. The questions above will be asked in a one-on-one interview and transcribed using a text tracker. The participant will answer the questions and be asked to explain in detail.
