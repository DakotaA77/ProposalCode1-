Introduction

The general area to be studied will be the impact of language usage. Studying the impact language has on individuals is important because language plays a large role in how we communicate within society and holds a lot of power.

Exclusive language can contribute to the harm of marginalized groups.I plan to specifically examine the way that gendered binary language, including generic language, excludes/impacts women and nonbinary individuals at Portland State University.
